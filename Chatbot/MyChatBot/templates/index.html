About MummyMate

MummyMate is an AI-powered pregnancy chatbot designed to provide personalized guidance, support, and reassurance throughout your pregnancy journey. Our chatbot is equipped with the latest medical knowledge and expert advice to help you make informed decisions about your health and your baby's well-being.Our goal is to empower you with the knowledge and confidence you need to navigate your pregnancy journey with ease. Whether you're a first-time mom or a seasoned parent, MummyMate is here to support you every step of the way.
